Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. No User Accounts

Plant Journal does not require or support user accounts. All plant data, journal entries, and photos are stored locally on your device. We have no access to your personal data.

2. Data Processing for AI

When using Premium AI features, the photo you select is sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. We do not store these photos on our servers. This data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Subscription Analytics

We use **RevenueCat** to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat collects anonymized transaction data (such as whether a subscription is active) to help us manage Premium features. No personally identifiable information (PII) is linked to this data by us.

4. Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and analytics.
  • Google Gemini API: AI plant identification.
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing.
