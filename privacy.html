Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

Wet Your Plants ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Wet Your Plants: Plant Journal mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Summary

Plant Journal stores your data locally on your device. Photos are only sent to third-party AI services when you explicitly use plant identification or diagnosis. We do not collect or store personal data on our servers.

Information Collection and Use

Plant Journal is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

Data Storage

Local Storage Only: All of your plant data, photos, notes, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

Camera and Photo Library Access

Plant Journal requires access to your device's camera and photo library to function properly:

  • Camera Access: Used to take photos of your plants for growth tracking and identification
  • Photo Library Access: Used to select existing photos from your device for plant identification, diagnosis, and adding to your plant journal
  • Photos you capture or select are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers

Notification Permissions

Plant Journal requests notification permissions to send you reminders about your plant care tasks (watering, misting, fertilizing, and taking photos). These notifications are generated locally on your device based on the schedules you set. No notification data is collected or transmitted.

AI Plant Identification or Diagnosis

When you use the AI plant identification or diagnosis feature (Premium feature):

  • Photos are sent to Google Gemini API for processing, plant identification, and diagnosis
  • We do not store these photos on our servers
  • Google's use of this data is governed by their privacy policy
  • The identification results are displayed to you but not stored by us
  • You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

Plant Journal offers a Premium subscription through Apple App Store and Google Play Store:

  • All payment processing is handled directly by Apple or Google
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Your subscription information is managed through your Apple ID or Google Play account
  • Apple and Google's privacy policies govern how they handle your payment data

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using device encryption and passcode protection
  • Backing up your device regularly to prevent data loss

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete individual plants, photos, or notes at any time within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device
  • We do not retain any data after you delete the app

Children's Privacy

Plant Journal does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Since all data is stored locally on the device, we have no access to user information regardless of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Third-Party Services

Plant Journal uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: For AI plant identification & Diagnosis (Premium feature only). Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: For in-app purchases on iOS. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Store: For in-app purchases on Android. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
